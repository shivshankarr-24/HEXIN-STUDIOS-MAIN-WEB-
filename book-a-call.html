01

How does the "Unlimited" design subscription actually work?

It’s straightforward. Once you subscribe, you can add as many design and website requests to your queue as you want. We work through them one by one, delivering premium, conversion-optimized assets. Once a task is approved, we instantly move to the next one. No hidden limits.

02

How fast will I actually get my designs?

On average, standard requests are delivered in 48 hours or less. Larger, more complex tasks—like a massive multi-page site overhaul or integrating your custom AI booking agent—are broken down into clear milestones so you are constantly seeing progress.

03

How does the AI Appointment Booking Agent work?

We integrate a smart, automated agent directly into your new website. It engages your traffic, handles the back-and-forth scheduling, and books qualified leads straight into your calendar 24/7. It eliminates the friction of manual booking, which is why it typically pays for itself within the first week.

04

What if I only need one website built and don't need ongoing optimization?

That is exactly why we offer the "Single Project" tier. We will clearly define the scope, give you a fixed timeline, and build your high-converting site from start to finish. If you ever need ongoing support later, you can always jump on the subscription.

05

What happens if I want to pause or cancel my subscription?

You have total control. We know businesses fluctuate. If you run out of design tasks for the month, you can simply pause your subscription and resume it when you have more work. No strict contracts, no penalties, and no questions asked.

06

Are your websites built from scratch, or are you just modifying cheap templates?

Every single build by Hexin Studios is completely bespoke. We design custom, premium UI/UX from the ground up in Figma based on your specific brand identity, and then develop it cleanly on platforms like Framer or Webflow. We do not use cookie-cutter templates—your site will be entirely unique to your business.

07

I’ve been burned by agencies that take months to deliver. What is your timeline?

We treat speed as a competitive advantage as we operate without traditional agency bloat, a standard high-converting storefront or portfolio project is typically completed within a few weeks. We break the project down into transparent milestones so you see progress in real time, ensuring we launch exactly on schedule.

08

What happens if I want to update text, images, or projects after the site is launched?

We build on modern, intuitive content management systems (CMS). During our seamless handover, we will guide you through a quick walkthrough showing you exactly how to edit images, portfolio projects, or text in seconds. You won't need to write a single line of code or pay a developer for basic day-to-day updates.

09

Do you handle the copywriting and SEO, or do I have to provide that?

You have total control. We know businesses fluctuate. If you run out of design tasks for the month, you can simply pause your subscription and resume it when you have more work. No strict contracts, no penalties, and no questions asked.
